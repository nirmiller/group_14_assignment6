The intended simulation depicts a rocket, exploding firework and firework fountain. 

Nir did the Rocket class has an initial velocity (thrust) and gravity that act on it. The rocket will shoot up at an 
angle and travel in a parabolic arc and when it reaches the top of that arc it disappears allowing for the firework to 
begin. This class has a initial velocity, position and texture. 

Meagan did the FireworkParticle class has an explosion force and gravity that act on the particles it creates. This 
class will randomly assign an angle the particle will go out at, a random rotation amount for the texture and randomly 
assign either a slower velocity or a faster velocity to a particle being made. The particles start positons are based on
the ending position of the rocket. The particles for one firework explosion will be made and stored in a list in Game1.
Meagan also added the looping for the rocket and firework (as they need to be looped together).  

Chris did the Fountain class has an initial velocity and gravity that act on the particles it creates. Fountain creates 
particles that have a position, velocity, life tracker, a max life and a spawn rate. A list of the fountain particles 
is created in Game1
